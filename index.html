§ 02 — Ethos Three points, held in tension

The studio runs on three quiet rules.

01 — Ship

Small ideas, fully realized.

We'd rather polish one small thing until it shines than half-build something large.

02 — Stay

Present in the work.

Craft comes from attention. Everything we make is a study in staying with it.

03 — Simplify

Remove what isn't essential.

Fewer features. Fewer metrics. Fewer meetings. More of the good part.

Let's make
something small.
